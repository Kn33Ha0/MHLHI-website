Our Story

MacArthur Highway Lumber & Hardware, Inc. started as a small retail shop in Meycauayan, Bulacan. Today, we serve as a primary hardware provider for the Bunlo community and beyond, specializing in electricals, tools, industrial fasteners, plumbing, construction supplies, and aggregates.

We bridge the gap between major suppliers and the local market, ensuring that contractors, business owners, and homeowners have consistent access to the high-quality materials they need to get the job done right.
